Recordings

Sometimes it can be hard to hear ourselves play whilst we are actually playing. This can be due
to varying levels of mental concentration - on the physical process of playing - distracting us from
the sounds being produced. The magnitude of this effect, naturally, depends on the difficulty of what
we are playing.

These recordings are here to enable you to listen to yourself playing - with complete attention devoted
to the sound - and to enable your parents and Sammy to follow your progress.
